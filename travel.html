    Kök Töbe (Kazakh: Көктөбе (төбе) Kök Töbe - "Blue Hill"; Russian: Кок Тюбе, Kok Tyube) is a mountain in Almaty, Kazakhstan's largest city. There is a popular recreation area on top of the mountain. The mountain's height is 1100 meters above sea level. Kök Töbe is one of the main landmarks in the city, and it is popular among visitors and tourists to Almaty.
